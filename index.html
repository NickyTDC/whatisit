The Dial Up Club

We Curate Professional Supper Clubs

The Dial Up Club is a professional networking community that brings together small groups of leaders, experts and educators through curated supper clubs built around shared challenges, industries and areas of expertise.

Join us for our launch evening

Just as dial-up internet was the original peer-to-peer connection, we're stripping professional networking back to its most fundamental purpose: putting the right people in the same room to build meaningful, lasting relationships.

Each supper club is designed for people who want more thoughtful professional relationships than traditional networking typically creates. If you've ever thought "I wish I could talk to more people who actually get what my job involves", this is designed with you in mind.

Why it exists

Professional networking works brilliantly for some people and barely at all for others. The Dial Up Club exists to close that gap, creating a platform where professional relationships are built intentionally rather than incidentally.

Each gathering brings together a cohort of twelve individuals who share similar levels of seniority, ambition and professional context, but who have taken completely different paths to get there. That difference is the point. It's where the most valuable perspectives emerge.

How the evenings are designed

Long before professional networking existed, humans built their most important relationships over food. Each Dial Up Club event takes place around a dinner table, hosted at carefully selected restaurants that champion exceptional food in a relaxed, welcoming setting.

Our supper clubs are capped at twelve attendees: enough people to generate genuinely diverse perspectives, but few enough for everyone to be heard and to contribute meaningfully to the discussion.

No conference rooms. No pitches. No selling. Just great food and thoughtful conversation.

We believe a great supper club doesn't just happen. It's curated. Our facilitation process begins before you arrive: we introduce attendees in advance so conversations can begin before the evening itself. During the event, we moderate a blend of structured and unstructured discussion, making sure every voice in the room is heard and that the conversation goes somewhere insightful.

And when the evening ends, we don't just wave you off. We actively support you in turning those connections into a network that lasts.

What our Individual Supper Clubs consist of

Twelve People

Large enough to generate diverse perspectives, small enough for every voice to contribute meaningfully.

Food and Venue

Every restaurant partner is personally selected, and personally loved, by our team. We choose spaces that champion exceptional food and effortless conversation.

Relevant Peers

Professionals drawn from different organisations but united by a shared professional theme.

Facilitation

An expert facilitator is present before, throughout and after the evening to ensure structure, pacing and that every voice is heard.

Chatham House Rules

What is said in the room stays in the room, creating the conditions for honest, substantive dialogue.

Pre-Event Introductions

Every attendee is introduced before the evening, so you arrive already knowing who will be at the table.

What to expect

Arrive already knowing who's in the room. Spend the evening in conversation that's purposeful but natural. Leave with a sense of who you'd like to stay in touch with, and the support to do so.

The details

The rhythm of each supper club is shaped by the group itself. Our ambition is for members to gather several times a year, with the cadence driven organically by the community.

For now, the Dial Up Club is invite-only. Every cohort is carefully assembled to ensure each gathering feels relevant, balanced and genuinely valuable. In time, membership will open to applications.
